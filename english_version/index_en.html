News of Our Movement

11.01.2026 - a seminar for Re-Haifa activists and volunteers to approve the movement's program of activities for 2026. It is planned to discuss activities to promote the food waste reduction program in Israel (2026-2035), the concept of Israel's transition to a circular economy (author's project), specific practices for the development of neighborly sharing, upcycling workshops, and other relevant areas.

26.12.2025 New Year Eco-Festival The ReHaifa Hub New Year Eco-Festival, in partnership with the Martef 23 community center and the Hadar community, was warm and truly large-scale: around 100 people joined us...

December 4, 2025 – Thursday at 17:00! The first meeting of Re-Haifa’s ecological community volunteers — we invite everyone who wants to join Haifa’s eco-community and help promote waste reduction initiatives. We’ll get to know each other in a fun, interactive way, talk about community plans — both immediate and long-term — and discuss how everyone can take part. We’ll be happy to see everyone who cares about making the city greener and reducing waste! ( HERE )
