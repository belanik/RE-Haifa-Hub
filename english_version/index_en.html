The RE Haifa movement is an association of residents of Haifa and the Krayot to share knowledge, experience, and resources for a sustainable future.

News of Our Movement

21.11.2025. A new initiative of our movement - an auction has been launched to raise funds for renting premises for Re-Haifa-hub. Join us! Take the things you need and give us the unclaimed, but worthy of auction - bring closer the moment of opening urban workshops for upcycling and reducing waste volumes ( HERE )

From 18.10 to 18.11.2025 we held the first Repair Month in Haifa in honor of International Repair Day — a pilot marathon that was included on the international map of the Open Repair Alliance. ( HERE )

08.11.2025 The local press is writing about us — so far, Russian-language, but this is only the beginning! ( HERE )

18.10.2025 ATTENTION! We are announcing an ECO-CHALLENGE in honor of International Repair Day - join and participate, your things are waiting for your attention and care! Details on our FB page. ( HERE )
