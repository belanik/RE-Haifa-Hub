News of Our Movement

11.01.2026 - a seminar for Re-Haifa activists and volunteers to approve the movement's program of activities for 2026. It is planned to discuss activities to promote the food waste reduction program in Israel (2026-2035), the concept of Israel's transition to a circular economy (author's project), specific practices for the development of neighborly sharing, upcycling workshops, and other relevant areas. )

26.12.2025 New Year Eco-Festival The ReHaifa Hub New Year Eco-Festival, in partnership with the Martef 23 community center and the Hadar community, was warm and truly large-scale: around 100 people joined us. In one day we ran five workshops for adults and kids—making denim New Year ornaments, creating decorations from old light bulbs, and turning plastic bags into bright decorative elements and craft materials—almost without any additional resources, using what is usually considered “waste”. We also hosted a swap with free finds (including tree ornaments and even a tree), held an eco-quiz with prizes, and wrapped up with a final auction. Special thanks to our partners: Dmitry Zotikov—for wooden toys made from the carpentry offcuts we provided—and Yulia Ivanter—for a mini-performance with puppets made from old textiles. Thank you to Martef 23, the Center for Social Justice, and our volunteer team—together we showed that a celebration can be bright without unnecessary shopping, through swapping, reuse, and shared creativity. )
