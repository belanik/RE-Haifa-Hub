News of Our Movement

ANNOUNCEMENT! The Re-Haifa Hub Eco-Center, together with Matnas Matref 23 and the Adar community, is organizing a New Year Eco-Festival: free market, workshops, quiz, and an auction starting from 1 shekel. All events are free, but donations are welcome!

December 4, 2025 – Thursday at 17:00! The first meeting of Re-Haifa’s ecological community volunteers — we invite everyone who wants to join Haifa’s eco-community and help promote waste reduction initiatives. We’ll get to know each other in a fun, interactive way, talk about community plans — both immediate and long-term — and discuss how everyone can take part. We’ll be happy to see everyone who cares about making the city greener and reducing waste! ( HERE )

November 21, 2025. A new initiative — we launched an auction to raise funds for renting a space for Re-Haifa Hub. Join in! Take what you need and donate unneeded but valuable items for the auction — help us open community workshops for upcycling and waste reduction! ( HERE )

From October 18 to November 18, 2025 we held Haifa’s first Repair Month in honor of International Repair Day — a pilot marathon that was featured on the international Open Repair Alliance map. ( HERE )

November 8, 2025 The local press wrote about us — so far in Russian, but it’s just the beginning! ( HERE )

October 18, 2025 ATTENTION! We announce an ECO CHALLENGE in honor of International Repair Day — join and participate! Your things are waiting for your care and attention! Details on our Facebook page. ( HERE )

September 29, 2025. The Re-Haifa Hub project caught media attention, and we were invited to Kan-Reka radio. You can listen to Bela Nikitina’s interview here (Good Morning, Israel — broadcast from September 29, 2025, starting at minute 22) ( HERE )

September 18–20, 2025 The “Local Hope” congress took place in Haifa, with most of our team participating in various events. Many new contacts, plans, and inspiration! Information about the organizer — the Center for Social Justice — HERE

September 16, 2025 Victory! We won first prize in the “Inspiring Project” category at the social business initiatives competition! (More information about the competition HERE)
